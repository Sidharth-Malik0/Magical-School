Great men that have helped Academy Of Wonders become such big of an education provider.

Mr.Necromar Shadowmance

Founder

The Academy of Wonders was founded in 1987 by Mr. Necromar Shadowmancer with the goal of providing a fun and engaging education for students. The school’s unique approach to learning combines traditional academic subjects with hands-on experiences and magical adventures, allowing students to explore their passions and develop their skills in a supportive and nurturing environment.

Mr.Mysticor Spellweaver

Chairman

In 2001, Mr. Mysticor Spellweaver was elected as Chairman of the Magic Based Education company. Under his leadership, the company has focused on providing innovative and engaging educational experiences for students, combining traditional academic subjects with hands-on learning and magical adventures. His vision and dedication have helped to make the Magicalcompany a leader in its field.

Mr.Aurorius Lightbringe

HOC

Since 2002, Mr. Aurorius Lightbringer has been the Head of Country (HOC) for our school. In this role, he has overseen the school’s operations and ensured it provides high-quality education to its students. Mr. Lightbringer’s leadership and vision have helped make our school a leader in its field, inspiring countless students to pursue their dreams and achieve their full potential.
